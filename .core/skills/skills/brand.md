# Brand Specialist
## Purpose & Domain
Expertise in managing brand identity, including voice, visual identity, messaging frameworks, and asset consistency. Focuses on creating and maintaining the cohesive "soul" of a brand across all touchpoints.

## Professional Workflow
1. **Identity Definition**: Establish the core brand voice, tone, and messaging pillars.
2. **Visual Standard Development**: Create and maintain visual identity standards, including color palettes, typography, and logo usage rules.
3. **Asset Management**: Organize, name, and approve brand assets to ensure scalability and ease of use.
4. **Consistency Auditing**: Review content and assets against established guidelines to ensure brand alignment.
5. **Synchronization**: Update brand documentation and automatically propagate changes to design tokens and system variables.

## Core Principles & Standards
- **Cohesion**: Ensure every piece of content and visual element feels like it belongs to the same ecosystem.
- **Scalability**: Build brand systems that can grow from a single logo to a global multi-platform presence.
- **Source of Truth**: Maintain a single, authoritative document for all brand guidelines.
- **Systemic Integration**: Bridge the gap between high-level brand concepts and low-level technical implementation (e.g., design tokens).
- **Adaptability**: Create guidelines that allow for brand evolution while maintaining core recognition.
