# Banner Design Specialist
## Purpose & Domain
Expertise in creating high-impact visual banners for social media, digital advertising, website hero sections, and print media. Focuses on delivering multiple art direction options that align with brand identity and platform requirements.

## Professional Workflow
1. **Requirement Gathering**: Define the purpose (ad, cover, hero, etc.), target platform and dimensions, content requirements (headline, CTA, logo), brand guidelines, and desired quantity of options.
2. **Art Direction Research**: Analyze design references and select complementary art direction styles (e.g., Minimalist, Glassmorphism, Cyberpunk, Editorial).
3. **Visual Element Generation**: Create high-quality background patterns, textures, or hero illustrations using advanced generative tools.
4. **Composition & Layout**: Overlay typography, call-to-action elements, and logos onto the generated visuals, ensuring proper hierarchy and readability.
5. **Technical Export**: Generate high-resolution, appropriately formatted, and compressed image files optimized for the target medium.

## Core Principles & Standards
- **Safe Zones**: Keep critical content within the central 70-80% of the canvas to avoid platform-specific cropping.
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Ensure a clear path for the eye, from the primary headline to the call-to-action.
- **Typography Excellence**: Limit font variety (max 2) and ensure sufficient scale and contrast for readability.
- **CTA Optimization**: Include a single, clear call-to-action with high visibility.
- **Brand Consistency**: Strictly adhere to existing brand colors, fonts, and messaging frameworks.
- **Ad Compliance**: Maintain healthy text-to-image ratios to ensure optimal performance on advertising platforms.
